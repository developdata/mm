ABOUT THE IDEA

The musical medic is a fun way of exploring how we feel and how music makes us feel better. Doctors provide medicine, we prescribe music. In a relaxed way we hope to provide music that transforms your mood from a negative to a positive, or positive to an even more positive, all the shades and directions of emotion.
