We prescribe music.

We are not medically trained but trained muscians who believe in the good music can do.

How it works

You, together with the Musical Medic , discuss how you deal with a heightened emotion of your choice. Together we develop a playlist of tunes that best helps you to feel comforted.This is the music that will make up your prescription, a list of music for you to take away.

In the moment , a piece of music from the playlist will then be played live!

Musical medics are musicians who want to share music in the hope that it connects people and helps them feel a bit better.

This is all free !
