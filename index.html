Hello

The idea behind musical medic is that we respond to music, music can help our mood.

Musical Medics aren’t medically trained. They’re musicians who want to share music in the hope that it connects with people and helps them feel a bit better.

The musical medic will talk with someone, and together they’ll come up with a kind of musical prescription—a playlist of music that feels right for them. Then the medic will play one of the tracks live.

We'll be

Popping up in different places, and where they’ll be will be posted on Bluesky Social.
